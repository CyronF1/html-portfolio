The Best Movies According to Cy

My top 3 movies of all time

Charlie's Angels

I love how the ladies/angels fight against their enemies. This movie definitely inspired me to become one of the angels.

Dead or Alive

This is another movie that revolves around girls who can fight. I am so in love with the action scenes and left me wanting to learn martial arts.

Hidden Figures

An amazing film that warms one's heart. This movie depicts the hardship encountered black women in NASA and how they strived to overcome racism.
